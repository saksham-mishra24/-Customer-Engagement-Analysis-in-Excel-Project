1. **Identify Key Metrics**:

   - Standard deviation.  
   - Sample size (n).  

2. **Apply Confidence Interval Formula**:  
   \[
   CI = \mu \pm Z \cdot \left(\frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}\right)
   \]  
   Where:  
   - \( \mu \): Sample mean  
   - \( \sigma \): Standard deviation  
   - \( Z \): Z-statistic for 95% confidence level (\( Z = 1.96 \))  
   - \( n \): Sample size
